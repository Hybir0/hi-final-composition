Why life is so good!



The definition of life has long been a challenge for scientists and philosophers.This is partially because life is a process, not a substance.This is complicated by a lack of knowledge of the characteristics of living entities, if any, that may have developed outside of Earth.
Philosophical definitions of life have also been put forward, with similar difficulties on how to distinguish living things from the non-living.
Legal definitions of life have also been described and debated, though these generally focus on the decision to declare a human dead, and the legal ramifications of this decision. As many as 123 definitions of life have been compiled.
